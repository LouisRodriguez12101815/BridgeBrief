Why BridgeBrief exists

Small B2B teams still rely on live outbound sales, but most lead lists are low-context and hard to work through. BridgeBrief gives the rep context first, then turns each lead into a structured call session with a clear next step.

Cold lists waste time

Reps dial leads with little context, weak prioritization, and no consistent follow-up workflow.

Calling costs add up fast

Outbound testing becomes expensive when teams over-dial low-quality leads or lose track of outcomes.

Context disappears after the call

Without a structured system, recordings, statuses, callbacks, and lead notes rarely stay organized.

How the workflow works

The MVP is intentionally simple: one trusted area code, one live rep, one short vetted queue, and a rep-first bridge pattern that is easy to test, easy to record, and easy to scale later.

1

Vet the lead batch

Select a small list of qualified leads to control cost and improve conversion quality.

2

Call the rep first

BridgeBrief calls the rep and reads a short lead briefing before any prospect is dialed.

3

Bridge the lead

The prospect is called from the trusted Aloha outbound number only after the rep is ready.

4

Store the outcome

Recordings, call statuses, and callback decisions map back to a DynamoDB-backed session history.
